⚖️ Maintenance Phase Plan

Learn how to maintain your physique and stay healthy after your transformation.

💪 Maintenance Guidelines

📌 Goal:

Maintain your current body composition, strength, and performance without gaining fat or losing muscle.

🏋️‍♂️ Workout Routine:

  • Train 3–5 times per week based on your preference and lifestyle
  • Focus on compound lifts to maintain strength (e.g., squat, deadlift, bench press)
  • Include mobility, flexibility, and core training
  • Cardio: 2–3 sessions/week for heart health and endurance

🍽️ Nutrition Guidelines:

  • Eat at caloric maintenance (calculate Total Daily Energy Expenditure - TDEE)
  • Macronutrients: balanced intake (Protein: 1.5–2g/kg, Carbs: moderate, Fats: healthy fats)
  • Prioritize whole foods: lean proteins, complex carbs, healthy fats, fruits, vegetables
  • Stay consistent with portion control and meal timing

🧠 Lifestyle & Habits:

  • Get 7–9 hours of quality sleep every night
  • Stay active: take walks, use stairs, stretch daily
  • Manage stress through mindfulness, hobbies, or journaling
  • Limit alcohol, ultra-processed foods, and added sugars

📊 Tracking & Accountability:

  • Weigh yourself once/week — fluctuations are normal
  • Take monthly progress pictures
  • Track strength levels — don’t let them drop significantly
  • Adjust food intake if weight consistently increases or decreases

✅ Bonus Tips:

  • Stick to a consistent schedule — routines prevent backsliding
  • Enjoy occasional treats without guilt — 80/20 rule
  • Stay connected to fitness communities or workout partners for motivation
  • Revisit your goals regularly — maybe it’s time for a new challenge!
